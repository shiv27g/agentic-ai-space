RAG Best Practices for Production

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems require careful design. This post covers document processing, chunking, and retrieval.

Document Processing

Use the right extractor: pdfplumber for tables, OCR for scanned docs. Validate extraction quality before chunking.

Chunking Strategy

Prefer semantic chunking over character-based. Preserve sentence and paragraph boundaries. Add 10-20% overlap.

Summary

Layer 1 (extraction) and Layer 2 (chunking) determine retrieval quality. Get these right before optimizing embeddings.
