Executive Summary

This report covers the financial performance of QuantumFlux Industries for fiscal year 2024. The company showed strong growth across all quarters, with Q4 revenue reaching $4.2 million, representing a 23% increase from Q3.

The primary revenue source continues to be enterprise software licenses, which accounted for 85% of total revenue. Customer acquisition efforts resulted in 78 new enterprise clients, while maintaining a 92% customer retention rate.

Looking ahead to 2025, we anticipate continued growth in the enterprise software market, with projected revenue of $5.5M for Q1 2025.
