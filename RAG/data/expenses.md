# expenses.md

## Reimbursement Timeline
Approved business expenses are reimbursed within 14 days of submission through the finance portal.

## Receipts
Receipts are required for any expense above 25 USD. Expenses without receipts above this threshold will not be reimbursed.

## Travel Booking
Flights and hotels booked through the company travel portal are billed directly to the company and do not require reimbursement.

## Per Diem
A daily per diem of 60 USD covers meals and incidentals during business travel.
