# benefits.md

## Health Insurance
All full-time employees receive comprehensive health insurance covering medical, dental, and vision from their first day of employment.

## Retirement Plan
The company matches retirement contributions up to 5 percent of base salary after six months of service.

## Learning Budget
Each employee has an annual learning budget of 1,000 USD for courses, books, and conferences related to their role.

## Wellness Program
The wellness program reimburses up to 50 USD per month for gym memberships or fitness apps.
