# support_sla.md

## Response Times
Priority 1 issues receive a response within 1 hour. Priority 2 within 4 hours, and Priority 3 within one business day.

## Escalation
Unresolved Priority 1 issues are escalated to the on-call engineering lead after two hours.

## Maintenance Windows
Scheduled maintenance occurs on the first Sunday of each month between 2:00 AM and 5:00 AM UTC.
