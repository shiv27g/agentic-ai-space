# leave_policy.md

## Sick Leave
All employees are entitled to 5 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave does not carry forward and is not encashable on exit. A medical certificate is required for absences longer than two days.

## Parental Leave
Employees with at least one year of service are entitled to 26 weeks of parental leave per child. Parental leave must be requested at least 30 days in advance and is separate from earned leave.

## Earned Leave
Earned leave accrues at 18 days per year after the first year of service. Up to 30 days of earned leave may be carried forward to the next year.

## Bereavement Leave
Employees may take 5 days of paid bereavement leave per event for the loss of an immediate family member.
