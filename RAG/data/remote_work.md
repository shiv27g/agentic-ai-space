# remote_work.md

## Remote Work Eligibility
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Fully remote arrangements require director sign-off.

## Core Hours
Core collaboration hours are 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM in the employee's local time zone. Meetings should be scheduled within core hours where possible.

## Home Office Stipend
Remote employees receive a one-time home office stipend of 300 USD for equipment such as a chair, desk, or monitor.
